Why outsource through Pistevo?



We are legally responsible in managing and coordinating all matters surrounding statutory requirements in the industry such as labour rights, taxation, Occupational Health and Safety, and other relevant policies set by the Labor Code of the Philippines.




Pistevo practically becomes an extension of your business - acting as one of your departments but a totally different entity. This is efficient because you can now focus on managing your business while we provide you with quality services.




You can rest assured that the labour resources we provide are of quality - not just competent and efficient, but also value their employment. We treat our employees right; they treat us and you right.




Pistevo aims to give Filipinos that burning confidence in the future. By recognising that the employer-employee relationship is a cycle of mutual benefit and dependence, the desire to serve you effectively becomes inherent to us.
